About EcoFresh

EcoFresh creates high-quality reusable bottles designed to replace single-use plastic in daily life. With a focus on safety, durability, and appealing aesthetics, we design bottles for families, athletes, students, and professionals.

Mission

To reduce single-use plastic pollution by making reusable bottle solutions affordable, attractive and widely available.

Vision

To be the most trusted reusable bottle brand in South Africa — recognized for quality, sustainability and community impact.

Materials & Quality

We use food-grade stainless steel, Tritan™ plastic for clarity and durability, powder coat finishes, and leak-tested lids. All products are BPA-free and pass our internal quality checks.
