Our Story

EcoFresh started with a simple goal: replace single-use plastic with bottles people love to carry every day. Now we help everyone get quality bottles for kids, gym-goers, travelers, and daily life.

What We Stand For

  • Sustainability: Reusable, recyclable materials.
  • Health: BPA-free and safe components.
  • Design: Practical, beautiful, long-lasting.

Materials & Quality

We use food-grade stainless steel or Tritan™ plastic, powder-coated finishes, and leak-proof lids tested to international standards.

The quality is so good that bottles can last 3 years without losing color or getting cracks.

Impact

Each bottle prevents hundreds of disposable bottles from entering landfills and oceans yearly.
