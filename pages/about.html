Our Story

EcoFresh began with a simple idea — design reusable bottles people feel proud to carry. We combine durable materials, clean manufacturing, and sustainable packaging to make a real reduction in single-use plastics.

What We Stand For

  • Sustainability: Reusable, recyclable materials and low-waste packaging.
  • Health: BPA-free plastics, food-grade stainless steel, safe finishes.
  • Design: Practical, beautiful and built to last.

Materials & Quality

We use 18/8 stainless steel, Tritan™ for clear bottles, and powder-coat finishes that resist chipping. Our lids are leak-tested and food-safe.

Impact

Every EcoFresh bottle can prevent hundreds of disposable bottles from reaching landfills and oceans over its lifetime.

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team blends design, production, marketing and customer care — committed to sustainability and quality.
