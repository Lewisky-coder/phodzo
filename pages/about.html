About EcoFresh Bottles

At EcoFresh Bottles, we believe hydration should never come at the cost of our planet. Our mission is to provide eco-friendly, reusable bottles that keep your drinks fresh while helping reduce plastic waste. Established in 2020, we’ve grown into one of South Africa’s trusted sustainable brands, promoting a cleaner, healthier future for all.

We combine innovation, design, and environmental responsibility — delivering bottles that are not just stylish but built to last. Whether you're hiking, studying, or working, EcoFresh ensures every sip you take is a step toward sustainability.

Our Vision

To inspire a lifestyle of sustainability by offering high-quality, reusable hydration products that make a positive impact on the planet.

Our Mission

We aim to eliminate single-use plastic bottles by promoting awareness, innovation, and community engagement.
