Frequently Asked Questions

Are EcoFresh bottles BPA-free?

Yes — all plastic parts are BPA-free and food-safe. Stainless steel models are food-grade 18/8.

How long do the bottles last?

With normal use and proper care, bottles can last several years. Avoid abrasive cleaners for painted finishes.

Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing?

Yes. Use our Enquiry form to request quotes for bulk orders, branding, or events.

Are bottles dishwasher safe?

Some models are dishwasher-safe (check product details). For powder-coated finishes we recommend hand washing to maintain colour.
